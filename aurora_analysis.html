Key Performance Findings

Adaptive Energy Management: System demonstrates 68% energy reduction when transitioning from urgent (1s) to relaxed (30s) deadline constraints, indicating intelligent resource allocation based on temporal requirements.
Dynamic FEC Optimization: Forward Error Correction overhead scales from 12% under high urgency to 3% under relaxed conditions, maintaining reliability while minimizing redundancy.
RIS Scalability: Linear SNR improvement (15dB to 28dB) with RIS tile scaling demonstrates effective spatial diversity utilization and intelligent surface configuration.
Perfect Reliability: 100% delivery success rate across all 42 test scenarios validates system robustness under varying operational constraints.
Duty Cycle Efficiency: Energy consumption inversely correlates with duty cycle budget, confirming effective power management and transmission scheduling optimization.
